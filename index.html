Resolution Limits

This model is mechanistic and evidence-based, but not literal single-neuron ground truth. Current human EEG/fMRI cannot resolve every neuron "down to the pixel." It estimates population-level activity and timing.

Evidence Anchors

  • Paired-click standard: S1-S2 = 500 ms.
  • P50 peak: ~50 ms cortical response window.
  • Late hippocampal gating contribution: ~250 ms.
  • Core network: STG, thalamus, DLPFC, hippocampus, cingulate/insula/claustrum.
  • Gating deficit in schizophrenia mainly reflects weaker S2 suppression.
